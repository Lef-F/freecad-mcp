## Notes

- Accessibility requirements apply at new construction
- At renovation (ändring): requirements may be adapted "skäligen" (reasonably)
- "Tillgänglig och användbar" = usable by persons with reduced mobility/orientation ability
- Handrail height: typically 0,90 m at ramps, 1,10 m at stairs (see stairs reference)
