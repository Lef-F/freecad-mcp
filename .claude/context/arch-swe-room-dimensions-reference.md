## Notes

- **Vistelserum** = rooms where people stay for more than a brief time (living, dining, bedroom, kitchen) — NOT bathrooms, storage, hallways
- Rumshöjd measured as free vertical distance from floor finish to underside of ceiling
- Sloped ceilings: the 2,10 m / 1,90 m applies where standing upright is needed
- Allmänt råd (non-binding): larger heights (2,50 m+) generally recommended for livability
